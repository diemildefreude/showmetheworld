show me the world

Interactive Artwork made by Stephan E Perez for the 2024 “Love Peace Harmony in Life Art & Music Festival” in MaeWang Sanctuary, Chiang Mai, Thailand. The festival is organized by Chumpol Taksapornchai and Juno's Jungle.

instructions

All aboard the World Train!

  • Press 'start audio' button and select your microphone
  • Select your language and press 'go!'. The train will depart.
  • Say 1 or 2 words into the microphone
  • The World Train will listen to you and take you in new directions
  • Watch as you ride past new people, places and things
  • Repeat!

notes

  • Sometimes the train will combine your words with older phrases
  • Sometimes Ganesha will appear. He looks very familiar...
  • The language can also be changed while riding the train:
    • E: English (en-US)
    • T: Thai (th-TH)
    • J: Japanese (ja-JP)
    • C: Chinese (zh-CN)
    • F: French (fr-FR)
    • D: German (de-DE)
  • High volume setting recommended.
  • Some iPhones might not display the motion lines
